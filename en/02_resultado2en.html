Master DC: Wuhan

Mathematical logic scales to any number of hubs. The centroid of a polygon is the average of its n vertices' coordinates. In this case, the exact logistics hub falls near Wuhan, known as the "Chicago of China" due to its role as the country's primary transportation hub.

/ Mathematical Key

For n=4 hubs with equal demand, we divide the sum of the coordinates by 4.

> Y-AXIS (Latitude):
Y = (Y1 + Y2 + Y3 + Y4) / 4
Y = (39.90 + 31.23 + 23.12 + 29.56) / 4
Y = 123.81 / 4 = 30.95
> X-AXIS (Longitude):
X = (X1 + X2 + X3 + X4) / 4
X = (116.40 + 121.47 + 113.26 + 106.55) / 4
X = 457.68 / 4 = 114.42
